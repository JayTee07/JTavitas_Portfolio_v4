The Cannoli Fund

Website Redesign

Role
  • User Research
  • Insights & Analysis
  • User Flow
  • UI Design
  • Wireframe & Prototype
In this project, I planned and conducted the interview with the founder of The Cannoli Fund, distilled the findings into an affinity diagram, developed a user flow for the prioritized feature of the website, contributed to the UI style guide, created lo-fi and hi-fi wireframes and prototypes.
Overview
Our bootcamp class was split into groups and given a project to select a local nonprofit and redesign their website. The Cannoli Fund is a nonprofit that operates in two neighborhoods south of downtown San Antonio, Texas specializing in reducing the feral cat population in the neighborhood. Their website was designed using a WordPress template that is no longer supported, causing usability issues on the their site.
The goal of the redesign was to identify and correct usability issues, and determine a feature to prioritize.
Timeline
We were given 3-weeks to complete and present the project.
Process
We followed the double diamond design thinking process which allowed us to stay flexible, collaborate and iterate on the project.
